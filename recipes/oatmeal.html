Ingredients

  • Mashed Banana
  • Baking Powder
  • Sweetener of Choice
  • Oats
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Cauliflower Rice
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Mash banana and mix with baking powder.
  2. Add sweetener, oats, and cauliflower rice.
  3. Add egg whites and milk.
  4. Mix all ingredients until fully mixed.
  5. Bake at 350F for 30-40 minutes or until baked through.
  6. Finish off with any desired toppings
