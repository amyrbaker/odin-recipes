Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Milk
  • Pancake Mix

Steps

  1. Mix the protein powder and pancake mix together in a bowl.
  2. Slowly add the milk.
  3. Mix until all ingredients are incorporated and no lumps remain.
  4. Heat an oiled pan ower medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter into a circle in the pan.
  6. Cook for about 2 minutes before checking edges and flipping to cook the other side for 1-2 minutes or until cooked through.
  7. Top with choice of toppings and enjoy!
